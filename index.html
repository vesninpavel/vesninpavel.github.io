ABOUT ME

I'm a 3D artist working in the game industry since 2010. I specialize in creating optimized, real-time assets for engines like Unreal and Unity — with a focus on clean topology, modularity, and production-ready materials.

My work blends clean modeling, smart UVs, and performance-aware materials with a love for small functional details.

My workflow is optimized, modular, and engine-ready.

Tailor's Workshop

This asset pack includes a collection of high-quality models themed around a tailor's workspace. Perfect for game environments and virtual sets.

Electrical Plugs

This asset pack contains over 18 different electrical plugs, covering regions such as Europe, the US, the UK, South Africa, and USSR-era designs. Each plug model comes in optimized low-poly versions with one LOD, ensuring flexibility for real-time applications while maintaining enough detail for close-up renders. These models are fully game-ready and suitable for a variety of uses, including VR environments, virtual households, and industrial design.

Survival: Siberia
